Kitchen Tips I've Learned

Here are some valuable lessons I've picked up along my cooking journey:

Mise en Place

Preparing and organizing all ingredients before cooking has revolutionized my kitchen workflow. It reduces stress and prevents mistakes when timing is critical.

Salt Early and Often

Properly seasoning food throughout the cooking process, rather than just at the end, creates depth of flavor that can't be achieved any other way.

Invest in Good Knives

A few high-quality, well-maintained knives make cooking more enjoyable and efficient than a drawer full of mediocre ones.
